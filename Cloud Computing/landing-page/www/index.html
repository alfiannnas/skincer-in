Revolutionizing Skin Health: Introducing SkinCer-In - The Future of Skin Cancer Detection

Bangkit Elevator Pitch is a great opportunity for the C23-PR568 group to participate in introducing an initial overview of the SkinCer-In application, an innovation in skin cancer detection. With enthusiasm and determination, we are ready to present an effective and easy-to-use solution for the early detection of skin cancer. SkinCer-In will combine the use of machine learning with advanced image processing technology, giving users the ability to perform their own skin analysis quickly and accurately. With our participation in the #BangkitElevatorPitch, we hope to garner attention and support to take this app to the next stage in eradicating skin cancer globally. Together, let's move forward and keep our skin healthy with SkinCer-In!
